ABOUT AIRDASHER

ANALYTICAL INTELLIGENCE FOR ROUTE DEVELOPMENT AND STRATEGIC HANDLING OF ECONOMIC RESOURCES

AIRDASHER is my submission for the R² Data Labs Responsible Growth Hack for the theme of "Identification of new air travel routes in emerging markets". This proposal makes use of several data sources like India-wide flight network dataset, several city-wise macro-economic factors & Indian railway connectivity dataset to identify airports having high demand & growth. The model then suggests different ways to include the city to an airline's network and also perform cost & resource analysis to judge feasibilty & profitability of such routes. Additionally, AIRDASHER can also help in identifying cities where new airports can potentially be added and also provide insights about the Indian & APAC flight connectivity network.
